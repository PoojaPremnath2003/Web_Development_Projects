About

DAV Sr. Secondary School, situated in Mogappair Chennai, is one of the leading schools in Tamil Nadu. Established in 1994, it is affliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education. It is part of the DAV Group of Schools Chennai, and is managed by the Tamil Nadu Arya Samaj Educational Society, Chennai.

My Experiences

I joined the school in June of 2012, in fourth grade.I graduated in 2021, amidst the COVID-19 Pandemic. I thoroughly enjoyed the nine years that I spent here, especially my high school years. I have made numerous friends, gained many valuable mentors and most importantly, spent most of my childhood years amidst an environment that has nurtured me into the adult I am today. My time at DAV has seen me go on yearly field trips, exhibitions, inter-house and inter-school competitions, and the hustle-bustle around my desk at lunchtime. Although my last year at school was spent in front of a laptop screen, the experience was no less in any sense, as even at home, I was surrounded by the support and guidance of my teachers.
